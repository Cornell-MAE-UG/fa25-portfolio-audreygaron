One problem from this course that I found most helpful and valuable was problem two on assignment three. This problem focused on one-dimensional heat conduction in a plane composite wall which has uniform volumetric heat generation. The goal of this problem was to calculate the right surface temperature of the second wall along with the uniform volumetric heat generation at steady state, the thermal conductivity of the first wall, and a plot of the temperature profile. I found this problem especially educational because each part required me to draw a different control volume around the composite structure. These control volumes each gave me certain pieces of information that I was then able to use to find the unknown for that portion of the problem. I also found this problem helped my learning as the plot drawn in part (d) illustrated how the heat rate equations are different for conduction and convection (parabolic vs linear).
